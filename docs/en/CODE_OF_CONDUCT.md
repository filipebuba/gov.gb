# Code of Conduct


Our goal is to maintain a collaborative, technical, and respectful community in **GOV-GB**.

### Commitment

Participants must contribute to an open and safe environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or role.

### Expected Behavior

- Clear, respectful, and professional communication.
- Technical criticism focused on facts and solutions.
- Openness to feedback and continuous learning.
- Collaboration aligned with the project's public-interest goals.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Offensive language or personal attacks.
- Improper exposure of sensitive data.
- Any conduct that harms community safety or integrity.

### Enforcement

Maintainers may apply proportionate actions, including:

- formal warning;
- temporary participation restriction;
- permanent removal in severe cases.
