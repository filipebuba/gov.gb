# Investors


This document summarizes **GOV-GB**'s value proposition for potential investors and impact partners.

### Thesis

GOV-GB aims to accelerate public digital transformation in Guinea-Bissau with focus on:

- inclusive digital identity;
- USSD-based access to public services (low-cost, low-connectivity);
- transparency through public metrics.

### Opportunity

- High potential for digitization of essential services.
- Strong demand for offline-first and mobile-first solutions.
- Room for sustainable digital public infrastructure models.

### Project Differentiators

- Modular architecture with working demos.
- Modern technical stack (Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase).
- Multi-language support aligned with local context.
- Focus on interoperability and institutional evolution.

### Collaboration Model

The project seeks investors and partners for:

- pilot funding and phased implementation;
- technical support in security, data, and operations;
- shared governance with local actors.

### Planned Use of Funds

- product and engineering evolution;
- security and observability hardening;
- field research and user validation;
- institutional enablement and rollout.
